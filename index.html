Operation announced on April 9th 2018
Operation ended on May 16th 2019

Following the Titanium Tank event, this community event featured 30 maps with 68 missions. Completing the campaign rewarded you with up to five medals, from rust to gold.

Exactly 100 contracts were available during the event, with the objective to complete these contracts and missions to earn enough stars to reach each medal tier.

All content creators who had a submission accepted into the campaign were eligible for the sixth, highest-tier medal: the Platinum Canteen.
